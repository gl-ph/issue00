ABOUT US

MAKER'S PLAYGROUND

In the spirit of works like Nick Montfort's Taroko Gorge, where makers are encouraged to reuse and recycle code to produce their own digital literature, gl-ph has built a Maker's Playground, where we make a work of digital literature available for download, reconfiguration, deformation and reupload. We encourage you to use these pieces as a starting point for creative and engaging work, and to send back to us whatever you create! We would love to see or host your work.
